Paris, France


Paris, often called the City of Light, is one of the most romantic and culturally rich destinations in the world. It is famous for its historic landmarks, world-class museums, charming streets, and exquisite cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the iconic symbol of Paris
  2. Louvre Museum – home to thousands of historic artworks
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture
  4. Montmartre – known for its artistic history and scenic views
  5. Seine River Cruise – a relaxing way to see the city

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – buttery and freshly baked
  • Crêpes – sweet or savory street food favorites
  • Macarons – colorful and delicate desserts
  • Coq au Vin – a classic French chicken dish
  • Cheese Platters – featuring a variety of French cheeses
